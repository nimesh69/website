Predicting Words from Videos Using BiLSTM Model

The utilization of the BiLSTM model for predicting words from videos involves several key steps. Initially, the video data is preprocessed to extract relevant features, such as frames or segments containing gestures or spoken words. These features are then converted into a suitable format for input into the BiLSTM model.

Predicting Words from Videos Using BiLSTM Model

In a video demonstrating sign language gestures, the BiLSTM model can accurately predict the corresponding words or phrases associated with each gesture, such as "What," "Good Morning," or "Bleeding." By leveraging its learned representations of sequential data, the model can generalize across different video contexts and accurately predict words from diverse input sources.
